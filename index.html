Black and Blueberry Farm

Berry status

​2021 Berry Season is over!! In season, (June and July) keep up with the current picking status (if berries are available) on our Facebook Page or by calling #417-396-8333.

Blueberries run from mid-June to early July

You Pick berries

Pick your own berries with buckets that are provided at the Picker Shack, on the north side of the barn. After you finish picking, return to the picker shack and your berries will be weighed.

We Pick berries

​We pick berries are berries that are picked for you. We pick berries are pre-ordered and when they are ready to be picked up you come by the farm and pay for them at the picker shack. Pay by the pound.

You Pick Lavender

​​You pick lavender is harvested from our lavender fields. Scissors are provided. A lavender bundle is between 75-125 stems or a handful. After cutting your lavender bundle there is a table in front of the greenhouse for arranging and twining your bouquet. Bundles are paid for in the barn.

Fresh Cut Flowers

There are designated flower beds for fresh flower cutting. Scissors are provided and fresh flowers are sold by the stem. Choose your favorite colored blooms and create a wonderful, personalized bouquet to take home. Mason jar vases are provided at an extra cost. Flowers are paid for in the barn.
